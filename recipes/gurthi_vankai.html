Description

Ingredients

  • Gurthi vankai- 1 kg
  • Onion - 1 big
  • Mirchi - 4
  • Ginger garlic paste- 3 spoons
  • Masala- 1 spoon

Steps

  1. First cut gurthi vankai like flower
  2. Grind onion ,masala,ginger garlic,salt,mirchi,karam until it forms a thick paste
  3. Now keep the masal paste inside gurthi vankai
  4. Now add oil in pan and cook gurthi vankai until it becomes soft
  5. Your Dish is ready to serve with Hot Rice
