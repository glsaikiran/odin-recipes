Description

Ingredients

  • Gongura- 2 kattalu
  • Onion - 1 big
  • Mirchi - 6

Steps

  1. First pick only the leaves of gongura
  2. Grind gongura keep it aside
  3. Now keep talimpu with mirchi and add gongura paste to it
  4. Now add sliced onions to it
  5. Your Dish is ready to serve with Hot Rice
