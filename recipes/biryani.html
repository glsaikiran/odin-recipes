Description

Ingredients

  • Biryani Rice- 1kg
  • Chicken - 1kg
  • Ginger garlic Paste- 3 spoons
  • Garam Masala 1-spoon
  • Curd- 1 cup

Steps

  1. First take rice boil upto 80% with salt and pinch masala
  2. Now add oil in biryani kadai add ginger garlic paste stir
  3. Add Chicken cook for 15 mins then check taste adjust it as per your taste
  4. Now add Rice,pudina and close lid with atta and cook for 15 min
  5. Now cook under tawa for 10 min and the dish is ready to serve with Raita
